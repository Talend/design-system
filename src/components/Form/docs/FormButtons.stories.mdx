### Buttons order

Sort the button by importance. From the less important to the more important.
- The primary button is placed on the right
- Avoid to use more than one primary button per form
- Other form buttons should be secondary buttons
- Depending on the container consider using a back or cancel button. This button should always be placed at the very left.
- The other action buttons are placed on the right side.
